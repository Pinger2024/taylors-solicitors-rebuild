Commercial Trading Disputes

We handle a wide variety of commercial disputes and related matters for clients based throughout the UK and Europe.

Resolving Business Disputes

We seek cost effective commercial solutions to clients' problems and advise on negotiation tactics with a view to avoiding litigation wherever possible.

We select the appropriate forum and utilise alternative dispute resolution techniques developing strategy and monitoring costs and progress.

Proven Track Record: We are confident with heavyweight cases, recently having completed a three month long trial - successfully. The firm regularly handles cases in the Court of Appeal and even the House of Lords.

Our Dispute Resolution Approach

We take a commercial and pragmatic approach to dispute resolution. Our first priority is always to understand your business objectives and the impact the dispute is having on your operations.

We then develop a strategy tailored to your specific circumstances, considering all available options including negotiation, mediation, arbitration, and litigation. Our goal is to achieve the best outcome for you in terms of both the result and the cost.
