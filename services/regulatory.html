Regulatory Investigations & Prosecutions

An increase in the number of clients involved in Trading Standard investigations and prosecutions has led to the establishment of a unit within our experienced Commercial Disputes Team.

Defending Your Business

This unit has been established to address the distress and disruption experienced by businesses facing investigation and prosecution by local authority regulatory bodies, particularly regarding Trading Standards matters.

Clients often provide information and submit to taped interviews (without first taking advice) when there is no legal requirement to do so, which makes it more difficult to defend or settle a subsequent prosecution.

Potential Penalties: Fines that can be imposed on conviction can be up to £5,000 per charge in the magistrates court, rising to £20,000 or unlimited per charge in the Crown Court - plus full enforcement costs recovery.
