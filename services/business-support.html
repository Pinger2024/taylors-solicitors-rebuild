Business Support & Restructuring

There are many reasons why a business, group of businesses or business within a group can find itself under pressure and under threat.

Specialist Restructuring Team

Businesses face destabilisation through competition, debt, inadequate working capital, or management issues, which manifest as cash pressure and deteriorating lender relationships.

The Taylors Business Support and Restructuring team comprises restructuring and recovery specialists in the areas of corporate law, commercial property, employment and dispute resolution.

Our approach is commercial, pragmatic, flexible and solutions driven. Businesses have been pulled back from the edge and are now growing and prospering as a result of that second chance.
