About Taylors

Why choose Taylors Solicitors? Because the firm was formed expressly to meet your needs by bringing a city service to your doorstep.

Our Story

City Experience, Local Commitment

The Partners and staff at Taylors Solicitors have all trained and gained experience in the major national and city law firms but have made a positive commitment to the area.

As the only wholly corporate law firm in the North West, at Taylors Solicitors we specialise in key commercial areas. As legal specialists, we are committed to providing the highest quality service through a real understanding of our clients' needs, business priorities and specific requirements.

We offer a common sense and commercial approach with which we deliver relevant, timely and commercially-astute, value for money solutions to the issues faced by businesses in the real world.

Our strength lies in our unrivalled experience, our business-like approach and our track record of providing timely, workable and cost-effective advice in a rapidly evolving business environment.

Not Just Legal Advice

We don't just give purely legal advice. Many of our clients look to us for general business advice drawing on the depth of experience which we have gained from working with clients over many years.

We understand that legal matters are just one part of the bigger picture for your business. That's why we take a holistic approach, considering the commercial implications of every decision and providing practical guidance that goes beyond the law.

Our Commitment

Ten Service Principles

The standards that guide everything we do

01

Speed of Response

We provide a faster, more responsive service, geared to the pace of the business world.

02

Relationships

We take time to develop close relationships with clients in order to understand their priorities, working methods and business relationships.

03

Simplicity

We make things quicker and easier by picking up the phone, calling at your offices or by a quick email rather than sending a long-winded letter.

04

Focusing

We focus on the business-critical issues in any transaction and not being distracted by issues which are peripheral to getting the deal done.

Attitude
